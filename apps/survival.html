Survival pipeline

The same engine on survival narratives — derive structured survival-logic, live, instead of brittle keyword-matching.

Apocalypse scenarios produce rich survival narratives that are usually parsed by hardcoded string-matching (includes("KIT WITHOUT")) — which breaks the moment a model rephrases. This swaps the logic rung for a causal one and recovers the structure: which survival appeals each narrative makes, the strategies they cluster into, and the live tension between them. It runs here in your browser.

[ TypeScript ] surface — survival appeals, evidence-pinned (no fixed keywords) [ Prolog ] logic — facts + rules; catches the live survival tension [ Lisp ] meta — clusters narratives bottom-up into strategies
