Obitel · operator-agent-loop

Derive the structure of a model's reasoning from what it actually says — live, in your browser.

Most analysis of model reasoning collapses it: a single score, or brittle keyword-matching against a fixed catalogue. This does the opposite — an intuition climbs a ladder of languages and the structure that returns is derived bottom-up from the text, not imposed. The logic below runs right here in your browser (TypeScript surface + a real Prolog engine + clustering); the full engine adds one flagship call at the very end. Understanding as compression, not brute force.
